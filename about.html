Hi, I'm Achenef Behulu Yibel

I'm a Computer Science Lecturer at the University of Gondar and a passionate researcher specializing in Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Natural Language Processing. With an MSc in Computer Science and a strong foundation in software development, I bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and practical applications.

My journey in technology began at Bahir Dar University, where I earned my BSc in Computer Science with a remarkable 3.76/4.00 GPA. This foundation led me to pursue my MSc at the University of Gondar, where I developed a deep learning-based classification system for traditional Ethiopian music ‚Äî a project that combines my technical skills with my passion for preserving cultural heritage.

Currently, I serve as a Lecturer at the University of Gondar, where I teach advanced computer science courses and supervise undergraduate research. I also work as a Junior Software Developer at Habtech Solutions PLC, developing healthcare analytics dashboards and ETL pipelines. My research interests span across multiple domains, and I'm particularly excited about applications of AI in healthcare, education, and cultural preservation.
