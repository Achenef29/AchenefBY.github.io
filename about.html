About Me

Get to know me better

Hi, I'm Achenef

I'm a passionate researcher and developer with expertise in machine learning, artificial intelligence, and software development. Currently pursuing my PhD in Computer Science at Stanford University, I focus on developing novel approaches for natural language processing and biomedical text mining.

My journey in tech began at Addis Ababa University where I discovered my love for solving complex problems through code. Since then, I've had the opportunity to work at leading tech companies like Google and Microsoft, contributing to cutting-edge AI systems that impact millions of users.

I believe in the power of technology to transform lives and am particularly interested in applications of AI in healthcare and education. When I'm not coding or researching, you can find me mentoring aspiring developers, contributing to open source projects, or exploring the intersection of technology and social good.
