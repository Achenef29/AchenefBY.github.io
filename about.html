Hi, I'm Achenef Behulu Yibel

My journey in technology began at Bahir Dar University, where I earned my BSc in Computer Science with a remarkable 3.76/4.00 GPA. Currently, I serve as a Lecturer at the University of Gondar, where I teach advanced computer science courses and supervise undergraduate research. I also work as a Junior Software Developer at Habtech Solutions PLC, developing healthcare analytics dashboards and ETL pipelines. My research interests span across multiple domains, and I'm particularly excited about applications of AI in healthcare, education, and cultural preservation.
